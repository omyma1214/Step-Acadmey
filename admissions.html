Admissions & How to Apply

Step 1: Admissions Open

Admissions are now open! Start your English learning journey with us today.

Step 2: Enter Your Email & Phone Number

Provide a valid email and mobile number for verification and communication.

Step 3: Fill Out the Admission Form

Complete the admission form with your personal and educational details.

Step 4: Generate and Pay Application Voucher

Download your payment voucher and pay the application fee online or at a bank.

Step 5: Confirm Your Admission

Once your payment is verified, your admission will be confirmed by email/SMS.

Step 6: Receive Student ID & Password

You will receive your student ID and password to log in to the portal.

Step 7: Login to the Student Portal

Use your credentials to access course materials, videos, and live sessions.

Step 8: Attend Online Classes

Start attending English classes, submit assignments, and take quizzes.
